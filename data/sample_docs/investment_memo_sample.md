## Executive Summary

Nordic Wind Farm Alpha is a flagship offshore wind project located in the Danish sector of the North Sea. The project involves the development, construction, and operation of an 800 MW offshore wind farm with a total capital expenditure of approximately EUR 2.4 billion.

## Key Risks

- **Permitting delays**: Medium risk, mitigation through early engagement with Danish Energy Agency
- **Supply chain**: Steel prices volatility affecting foundation and tower costs
- **Grid connection**: Timeline dependent on Energinet (Danish TSO) approval
- **Weather windows**: Construction schedule sensitive to North Sea weather conditions

## Financial Projections

- Expected P50 net capacity factor: 48%
- Annual energy production: ~3,360 GWh
- Revenue model: 60% PPA, 40% merchant exposure
- Debt-to-equity ratio: 70/30
- Target equity IRR: 8-10% (unlevered)

## ESG Considerations

- GRESB rating: A+
- Estimated CO2 avoidance: ~1.5 million tonnes per year
- Local community benefit fund: EUR 2 million annually
- Biodiversity impact assessment completed with mitigation measures in place
